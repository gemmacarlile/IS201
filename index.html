Positions in an Eight-Man

Different positions bring different qualities to the table...

  • Seat 1 typically has the best balance
  • Seats 3 & 4 typically have the worst technique
  • Seats 5 & 6 are the strongest
  • Seat 8 sets the pace, and is the most technically talented
